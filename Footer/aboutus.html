About Us

Fashion Walk is a leading fashion retailer offering compelling clothing, shoes and accessories for men, women and kids. Since 2000, we've been
committed to providing our customers with the best possible service and to improving it every day.

We believe fashion is a business of optimism, and in that spirit we continue to grow and evolve.

Fashion changes. Shopping changes. Our commitment to happy customers doesn't.

Our History

Fashion Walk was founded in 2000 with a simple idea — make it easier to find a pair of jeans that fit with a commitment to do more.
In the past 20 years the company has grown into several brands.
